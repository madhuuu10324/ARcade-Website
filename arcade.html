ARCADE'24
SRM ARcade hosts a one-of-a-kind symposium for school students, blending traditional arcades with Augmented Reality. Bridging the gap between technical and non-technical domains, the event combines cutting-edge technology with classic arcade charm for a creative, engaging, and nostalgic experience. The symposium promises to inspire a future filled with excitement and discovery.
